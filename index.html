A meticulously restored heritage building in a former godown on the bank if the Singapore River in Robertson Quay

RIVER VIEW SUITE

The River View Suite is our largest, open plan room designed in loft style with ample historic conservation detailing including original beams and a soaring peaked ceiling. It is located on the top floor of the building and looks out over the Singapore River. The suite is fitted with a super king-sized bed, ensuite bathroom with standalone bathtub, and spacious living area.

RIVER VIEW MEZZANINE

The River View Mezzanine is a unique room that features two levels, a reading library, and a view over the Singapore River. One of our largest rooms in the building, it has a set of stairs to go up the second level and ample historic industrial character, including original beams, windows, and a peaked ceiling. It is on the top floor of the building and is fitted with a kingsized bed and ensuite bathroom
